Relations

What is a relation?

A relation is how the elements in the domain relate to the elements in the codomain.
Domain of the relation - the "subject" of the relation.
Co-domain of the relation - the "object" of the relation.

Elements of the Relation
Suppose the domain is {Matt, Ethan, Jed} and the co-domain is {Cat, Dog, Lion}
The ordered pairs of the domain and co-domain are (Matt, Cat) , (Ethan, Dog) , and (Jed, Lion)
We call these the elements of the relation. Displaying Elements in a Relation One way of displaying a relation is using a picture.
